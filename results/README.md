# Z-Score Clustering of CV Top 200 Genes + Pattern Visualization

**Interpretation:** The gene expression heatmap shows mostly that genes are downregulated with heat stress and upregulated with cold across the yeast cultivars. Cluster 1 shows the strongest response to heat stress through the most extreme Z-score values and also shows the quickest response with only 5 minutes of room temperature after heat stress it began to downregulate its genes. Cluster 2 shows less response to heat stress in the 5 minutes of room temperature after heat, suggesting its expression of heat stress related genes was not as great, and Cluster 2 showed more of a prolonged response going from room temperature to 37 degrees celsius. Cluster 3 was the least sensitive to changes in temperature. Cluster 4 had several trends that were unexpected in the treatments that lasted a long time where its expression was higher when going from room temperature to 37 degrees celsius instead of lower like the others.
